# Test Your System With These RFPs

## Simple:

`Hi, we're looking to book a meeting room for our quarterly board meeting. 12 attendees, full day on May 15th. We'll need lunch, a projector, and coffee service throughout the day. Budget around EUR 2,000.`

## Medium:

`We're organizing a 2-day product launch event for approximately 80 guests. Day 1: full-day conference with a main stage and 2 breakout rooms, lunch and afternoon coffee break. Day 2: half-day workshops (3 parallel tracks, 25 people each) followed by a networking lunch. We need AV equipment in all rooms, high-speed WiFi, and a registration desk. Dates are flexible in June. Budget EUR 15,000–20,000.`

## Complex:

```Planning a 3-day destination wedding, June 20–22. 120 guests total.

Day 1: Welcome dinner for 50 (rehearsal dinner, informal).

Day 2: Ceremony (outdoor preferred, indoor backup for 120), cocktail reception (120), seated dinner (120) with live band, open bar until midnight.

Day 3: Farewell brunch for 80.

We'll need: 40 hotel rooms for 2 nights (mix of standard and suites, bridal suite required), spa access for bridal party (6 people), floral arrangements for ceremony and dinner, dietary accommodations (8 vegetarian, 3 gluten-free, 2 vegan). Budget EUR 45,000–60,000 excluding accommodation.```
